Chhole Bhature

Description:

It is a combination of chana masala (spicy white chickpeas) and bhatura/puri, a deep-fried bread made from maida.

Chole bhature is often eaten as a breakfast dish, sometimes accompanied with lassi.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cup soaked chickpeas
  • 2 chopped onion
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp turmeric
  • 1tbsp ginger
  • 1/2 tbsp chana masala
  • 1 tbsp garam masala powder
  • 3 tbsp onion paste
  • Salt as required
