Fried Rice

Description:

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.

It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients:

  • Chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 3 cups white rice
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil

Steps:

  1. Assemble Ingredients.
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds.
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
